Design Process

1

Emphatize

Understand the needs and wants of the user by gathering information through observation, interviews, and research.

2

Define

Clearly define the problem or opportunity by synthesizing the information gathered in the empathize stage.

3

Ideate

Generate a wide range of ideas to solve the problem or capitalize on the opportunity. Encourage creativity and don't judge ideas at this stage.

4

Prototype

Create a physical or digital representation of the most promising ideas. The prototype should be simple and inexpensive to create.

5

Test

Gather feedback from users on the prototype, and use it to iterate and improve the design. Repeat the process as necessary until a satisfactory solution is found.
